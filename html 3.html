How can you contribute to making a better environment?

Reduce plastic waste at home:

Recycle plastics, use alternatives to plastic like reusable utensils, grocery bags, etc. Shop locally and buy fruit & vegetables not wrapped in plastic.

Do not use the toilet as a trash bin:

Avoid flushing wet wipes, diapers, and other things that cross your mind down the toilet, that is what a trash bin is for. Flushing these items down the toilet can cause swage lines to become blocked. When things get blocked, water cannot be cleaned efficiently.

Education:

Adults can teach children about the value of clean water and the consequences of water pollution, helping to cultivate environmentally responsible habits for future generations.

Donating money:

To combat water contamination is a powerful way to contribute to solutions on a global, national, or local scale. Clean Water Action is working to protect our water resources, reduce pollution, and ensure everyone has access to safe water. Your donation helps fund crucial campaigns and solutions to protect our water systems. Please visit cleanwater.org to donate and support their important work!
